Frequently Asked Questions

Which engine is better for beginners - Unity or Unreal? +

We start with Unity as it's more beginner-friendly with C# programming. Later we introduce Unreal Engine to give you exposure to both major engines, making you more versatile in the job market.

Do I need a powerful computer for game development? +

A mid-range computer with dedicated graphics card is recommended for smooth development. However, we provide optimization techniques to work on less powerful machines during learning.

Can I really get a job after this 10-week course? +

Absolutely! Our graduates have secured positions at gaming studios, work as freelancers, or start their own indie projects. The 4 completed game prototypes in your portfolio demonstrate real skills to employers.

What about 3D modeling and art assets? +

We teach basic 3D modeling with Blender and provide access to asset stores. For complex assets, we show you how to source or commission them professionally.

Is there ongoing support after course completion? +

Yes! You get lifetime access to course updates, the alumni community, and continued mentorship. We also provide job placement assistance for 6 months after graduation.

What programming languages will I learn? +

Primarily C# for Unity development, with introduction to C++ concepts for Unreal Engine. We focus on game programming patterns that apply across languages.
