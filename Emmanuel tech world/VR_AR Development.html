Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a VR headset to take this course? +

A headset is recommended but not required. We develop for multiple platforms including mobile AR, and provide simulation options for VR development without physical hardware.

What computer specifications are needed? +

Recommended: Windows 10/11, Intel i7/Ryzen 7, 16GB RAM, RTX 3060/RTX 4060 GPU. Minimum: i5/Ryzen 5, 8GB RAM, GTX 1060 GPU for basic development.

Which VR platforms will we target? +

We focus on Meta Quest 2/3 as the most accessible platform, plus PC VR (SteamVR) and mobile AR (iOS/Android). Skills transfer across all XR platforms.

Can I get a job in VR/AR after this course? +

Absolutely! The XR industry is growing rapidly. Our graduates work in gaming, enterprise training, architecture visualization, and healthcare applications.

What programming language will we use? +

Primarily C# with Unity. We also cover shader programming basics and platform-specific SDK integration.

Is 3D modeling experience required? +

No, we provide 3D assets and teach basic 3D concepts. However, 3D modeling skills are beneficial and we cover integration workflows.
