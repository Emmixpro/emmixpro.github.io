Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need artistic talent to become a graphic designer? +

While creativity helps, graphic design is more about understanding principles and using tools effectively than raw artistic talent. We teach systematic approaches that anyone can learn regardless of artistic background.

What software will I need for this course? +

We primarily use Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) which offers student discounts, plus free alternatives like Figma and Canva. All software we use either has free trials or affordable student pricing.

Can I learn graphic design without a drawing background? +

Absolutely! Most professional graphic designers use digital tools rather than hand-drawing. We focus on digital design techniques that don't require traditional drawing skills.

What computer specifications do I need? +

A modern computer with 8GB RAM minimum (16GB recommended), decent processor, and adequate storage. Both Windows and Mac work perfectly for design work.
