Our Story

Founded 2015

Emmanuel's Tech World began with a simple mission: to make quality tech education accessible and practical for everyone. Frustrated by traditional education that focused too much on theory and not enough on real-world skills, Emmanuel started teaching practical tech skills through WhatsApp.

"We focus on project-based learning, mentorship, and real-world tools that employers actually want. Each course includes hands-on projects, mentorship sessions, and career support. We keep content current with industry best practices."

What started as teaching a few students has grown into a thriving community of learners across Nigeria and beyond. Our WhatsApp-first approach has proven to be more effective, personal, and accessible than traditional online platforms.
