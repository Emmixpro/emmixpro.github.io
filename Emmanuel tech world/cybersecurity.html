Frequently Asked Questions

Is cybersecurity only for technical people? +

While technical skills are important, cybersecurity also needs analytical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. We teach both technical and soft skills needed for success.

What's the difference between ethical hacking and cybersecurity? +

Ethical hacking (penetration testing) is a subset of cybersecurity focused on finding vulnerabilities, while cybersecurity includes broader protection, monitoring, and incident response.

Do I need programming knowledge for cybersecurity? +

Basic scripting knowledge is helpful but not required to start. We cover essential Python and Bash scripting for security automation during the course.
