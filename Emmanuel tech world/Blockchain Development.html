Frequently Asked Questions

Will we deploy to mainnet and need real cryptocurrency? +

We primarily use testnets (Goerli, Sepolia) for free development. Mainnet deployment is optional and we provide guidance for those who want to deploy with real ETH (~$50-100 worth for gas fees).

What programming experience do I need? +

Basic JavaScript knowledge is recommended. We teach Solidity from scratch and provide JavaScript refresher materials for web development.

Can I get a blockchain developer job after this course? +

Yes! Blockchain developers are in extremely high demand. Our graduates secure roles at Web3 companies, DeFi protocols, and NFT platforms with competitive salaries.
